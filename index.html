DO YOU KNOW
NARUTO..??

Naruto Uzumaki is a fictional ninja from the popular manga and anime series of the same name. He is the protagonist who was shunned by his village because the Nine-Tailed Fox was sealed inside him at birth.Despite this, he strives to become the leader of his village, the Hokage, to gain recognition and respect.

DO YOU KNOW
HINATA..??

Hinata can refer to several characters, most famously Hinata Hyuga from the Naruto franchise and Shoyo Hinata from Haikyuu!!. Hinata Hyuga is a kind, shy kunoichi from the Hyuga clan who marries Naruto Uzumaki, while Shoyo Hinata is a determined and energetic volleyball player who dreams of becoming a top athlete.
